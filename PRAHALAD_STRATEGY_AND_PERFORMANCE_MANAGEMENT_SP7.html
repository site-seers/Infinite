STRATEGY EXECUTION USING THE BALANCED SCORECARD

Effective strategy implementation remains one of the greatest challenges institutions face. It is more difficult than formulating a good strategic road map. Four main barriers stand in the way: vision barrier, people barrier, management barrier and resource barrier. A bad strategy, however clinically executed, leads the entity into intensive care unit; a good strategy characterized with the barrier cited takes the entity into high dependency unit. A bad strategy wrapped in the four barriers sends the entity to the corporate morgue. Entities tend to overcomplicate strategy, under-communicate and get imprisoned by the inherent structural conflict between strategy and organizational design and dissonance between long term planning and operational issues.
